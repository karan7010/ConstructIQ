# Construction Site Safety & Material Standards (Sample)
## Masonry Work (IS 2212)
- Bricks should be soaked in clean water for at least 2 hours before use.
- Mortar for load-bearing walls should be 1:6 (Cement:Sand).
- Verticality of walls must be checked every 1 meter of height.

## RCC Standards (IS 456)
- Concrete cover for beams should be 25mm.
- Curing for RCC slabs must continue for at least 7 days.
- Maximum drop height for concrete during pouring is 1.5 meters.
